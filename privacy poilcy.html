If a user gets identified as a child under this prescribed age within the Services, Vannala Mobile Apps will not collect, store or use any information of such user.
Vannala Mobile Apps also does not allow third party partners to access personally identifiable data from users under 13.
In addition, we may limit how we collect, use, and store some of the information of EU users between 13 and 16. The personal data have been collected in relation to the offer of information society services. Please note that your right to erasure may be limited if the data are necessary for compliance with a legal obligation or for the defense of legal claims.
